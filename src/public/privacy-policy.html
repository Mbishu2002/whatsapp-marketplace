Welcome to the WhatsApp Marketplace ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience when using our service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • WhatsApp Information: When you use our service, we collect your WhatsApp phone number, profile name, and messages you send in groups where our bot is present.
  • Listing Information: When you post a product or service for sale, we collect details about your listing including title, description, price, location, and any images you share.
  • Transaction Information: If you make or receive payments through our platform, we collect transaction details, payment status, and related communication.
  • Usage Information: We collect information about how you interact with our service, including search queries, viewed listings, and user preferences.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our marketplace services
  • Process and facilitate transactions between buyers and sellers

  • Detect and prevent fraud, spam, and abuse
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our service

3. Information Sharing

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Between Users: When you post a listing, your listing information and contact details are shared with potential buyers.

  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

4. Data Storage and Security

We store your data on secure servers and implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • Access to your personal information
  • Correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion of your information
  • Restriction of processing
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

6. WhatsApp Data Usage

Our service operates on WhatsApp and adheres to WhatsApp's Terms of Service. We do not use your WhatsApp data for purposes beyond those described in this Privacy Policy. Our bot only reads messages in groups where it has been explicitly added and configured for monitoring.
