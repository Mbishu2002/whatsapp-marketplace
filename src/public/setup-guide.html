1

Save the Bot's Phone Number

Add our marketplace bot's phone number to your contacts. This is the number shown above.

Tip: Name the contact something memorable like "Marketplace Bot" so you can easily find it later.
2

Create or Choose a WhatsApp Group

You can either:

  • Create a new WhatsApp group for marketplace listings
  • Use an existing group where you want to enable marketplace features
Tip: Groups with specific categories (e.g., "Electronics Marketplace" or "Fashion Items") work best!
3

Add the Bot to Your Group

Open your WhatsApp group, tap the group name at the top, then:

  1. Scroll down and tap "Add participant"
  2. Find and select the marketplace bot contact you saved earlier
  3. Tap the checkmark or "Add" button to confirm
4

Register Your Group

Send the following message in your group:

!register [category]
Example: !register electronics

Available categories: electronics, fashion, vehicles, property, services, jobs, furniture, other

The bot will confirm when your group has been registered successfully.

5

Start Posting Listings!

Now you can post product listings in your group. The bot will automatically detect and extract listing information.

A good listing format includes:

  • Clear product title
  • Price
  • Description
  • Location
  • Photos of the item
Example:
FOR SALE: Samsung Galaxy S22
Price: 350,000 FCFA
Condition: Used (9/10)
Location: Douala
Description: 8GB RAM, 128GB storage, comes with charger and case. No scratches.
Contact: Already included in your WhatsApp profile
6

Search for Products

To search for products across all marketplace groups, start a private chat with the bot and send a message like:

search [product name]
Example: search iphone
